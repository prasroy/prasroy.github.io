How I Structure Side Projects

Side projects fail when scope grows faster than momentum. I now start with one narrow outcome and a one-week delivery target.

My baseline process is simple: define one user problem, ship the first usable version quickly, and publish a short postmortem.

This creates a repeatable loop: idea, execution, reflection, iteration. Over time, small cycles compound into meaningful progress.
